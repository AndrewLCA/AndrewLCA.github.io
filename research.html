Research Focus

Designed and implemented a C++ matrix multiplication algorithm optimized for globally sparse, locally dense matrix structures.

What I Worked On

  • Designed a hybrid matrix multiplication approach for structured sparse workloads
  • Studied how local dense tiles can be exploited inside globally sparse matrices
  • Benchmarked performance across different sparsity regimes and hardware settings
  • Compared results against Intel oneAPI MKL and cuBLAS HGEMM baselines
